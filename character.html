MAIN CHARACTER

SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is a fun-loving, optimistic sea sponge who works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab. His cheerful attitude and enthusiasm get him into many hilarious situations.

Patrick Star

Patrick is SpongeBob’s best friend and is known for his childlike, naive personality. He is carefree, often lazy, and doesn’t always understand the world around him.

Squidward Tentacles

Squidward is SpongeBob’s co-worker and neighbor. He is often grumpy, sarcastic, and pretentious, with a love for art and playing the clarinet.

Mr. Krabs

Mr. Krabs is the money-obsessed owner of the Krusty Krab. He cares deeply for his daughter, Pearl, and occasionally shows concern for his employees, though he is often greedy and selfish.

Sandy Cheeks

Sandy is a squirrel from Texas who lives underwater in an air dome. She is highly intelligent, skilled in karate, and is a good friend of SpongeBob and Patrick.

Plankton

Plankton is the tiny, evil mastermind behind the Chum Bucket, a competitor to the Krusty Krab. His main goal is to steal the Krabby Patty formula.

Pearl Krabs

Pearl is Mr. Krabs' teenage daughter, a whale with a bubbly and caring personality. She loves shopping and is very different from her money-hungry father.

Gary the Snail

Gary is SpongeBob’s pet snail, often compared to a cat. He communicates with meows and is very intelligent, though he rarely speaks.

Larry the Lobster

Larry is a muscular lobster who is popular at the beach and loves bodybuilding. He often helps out the other characters with his strength and athleticism.

Mrs. Puff

Mrs. Puff is a pufferfish and SpongeBob’s boating school instructor. She is patient but often ends up inflating herself out of fear when SpongeBob’s antics cause problems.

Flying Dutchman

The Flying Dutchman is a ghostly pirate who haunts the seven seas. He is known for his terrifying appearance and his role as a villain in several episodes.

Karen

Karen is a computer who is married to Plankton. She assists him with his evil schemes, though she often mocks his failures and shows more intelligence than her husband.
